title: Provider Selection
description: Choose the provider that matches your product model, runtime constraints, and regional requirements without painting the rest of the codebase into a corner.
---

## Start from product shape, not brand familiarity

Choose the provider based on what your billing flow actually needs.

### Pick Stripe when

- you want the broadest first-party payment surface in this repository
- you need native PIX support today
- you want the most mature checkout and customer support in the current codebase

### Pick Polar when

- your checkout model is product-driven
- you are comfortable supplying product ids directly
- you do not need native PIX from the provider

## What does not change

When you switch providers, your application code should still mostly talk to:

- `paymesh.payments`
- `paymesh.customers`
- `paymesh.webhooks`

The point of provider selection in Paymesh is not to eliminate every provider difference. It is to make those differences local.
